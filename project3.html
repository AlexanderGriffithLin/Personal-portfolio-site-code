Project 3:
App Prototyping

Project Description

This group project was for my first year interaction and visual design module. The aim was to create a high fidelity prototype for a smartphone application based on psychololgical concepts we had researched. This was also a group project I conducted with two of my classmates, Grace Lawless and Sian Lavery.

Our Approach

Together our group formulated and prototyped an application for an app for helping parents and expecting parents named Bump and Beyond. We created paper prototypes and personas to detail how the app should be designed, and then we designed the app in the software Figma. The documentation can be accessed below.
This project was my first time entering the world of app development and it was very interesting to see how that process could unfold. I gained experience in creating personas, paper prototypes, and in using Figma to create high fidelity prototypes. I also gained valuable experience in working in a team in an effective and fair manner.
