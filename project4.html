Project 4:
Observation Study

Project Description

This project was for my second year child development module. The aim was to learn how to conduct a child observation correctly and describe how the experience went through the medium of a poster.

My Approach

I conducted the observation on a family member's toddler after gaining consent from the parents. The observation was then compared the the present literature, upon which I concluded that the child was not deviant from the norm in any way compared to other children his age. Then, using Affinity Photo 2, I formualted my findings in the form of a poster.
This project gave me extremely valuable insight in how to conduct a child observation in an ethical and productive manner, as well as further experience in Affinity Photo 2.
