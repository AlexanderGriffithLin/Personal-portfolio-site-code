Project 2:
Game Analysis

Project Description

This project was for my first year cyberpsychology module. The aim was to apply the findings of a journal article to a video game and create a video presentation describing the research and the game.

My Approach

Huang et al.

My Approach

I read through the paper Huang et al. (2022), accessible below, and researched the theories they discussed, goal gradient theory and flow theory, in depth. Then I examined the video game Team Fortress 2 and how it related to the paper. Then using the software OpenShot to show my findings in video format.
