Project 6:
Volunteering

Project Description

A currently ongoing project for my third year social psychology altrusim project is my volunteering work with LauraLynn, the children's hospice and disability respite centre. I signed up with the buddy program, where once a week I visit the grounds of LauraLynn and spend some time with one of the residents, which primarily takes the form of going on walks with them. It has been a wonderful and greatly fulfilling experience so far.
