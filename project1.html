Project 1:
Poster Design

Project Description

This project was for my first year psychology of design module. The aim was to prototype and create a series of posters for fake psychology confrences keeping basic psychological concepts, such as the Gestalt principles of design, and design concepts, such as proper typography, in mind to create aesthetically pleasing posters.
Prototyping and documentation was conducted using the software Miro, and the posters were created using Affinity Photo 2.

My Approach

After spending some time understanding the ins and outs of Affinity Photo 2, I created three prototypes using pen and paper based on the works of Rorschach, Bandura, and Lacan. I then created the three posters over the course of a few weeks, creating extensive documentation of all steps on Miro. This assignment gave me great insight into how to use image editing software, as well as experience in how to apply design principles (miro password: Psychology).
