Project 5:
Film Analysis

Project Description

This project was for my first year interaction and visual design module. The aim was to critically examine a film that portrays a mental illness and compare it to how said mental illness is described in a diagnostic manual. Then the findings had to be presented in video format.

My Approach

I chose the award winning film "The Father" and compared it's depiction of dementia to the diagnostic criteria for dementia as described in the ICD-11. I watched the film many times, read up on multiple scholalry papers concerning dementia, and created a script describing how the film and the IC-11 compare. Then I worked on a video detailing my findings, created using the software OpenShot, where I read out my script.
This project gave me valuable knowledge in how to read and understand diagnostic manuals and criteria, as well as giving further insight into the software OpenShot.
